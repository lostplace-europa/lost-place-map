Our partners Our proud partner is Innovario.org, a community platform for exploring lost places and adventures.

Lost places and collaboration Together with Innovario.org, we promote the exploration of lost places and the sharing of adventures, photos and stories. We work closely together to document new lost places and create exciting content.

Lost Places in regions Innovario.org presents Lost Places from different regions and offers maps, photos and information on access options. This allows you to discover exciting places in your area and share them with others.

Community The Innovario.org community thrives on exchange and mutual support. Here you can ask questions, get tips and find like-minded people who enjoy lost-place adventures as much as you do.

You can find more information here: www.innovario.org
